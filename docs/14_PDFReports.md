# 14_PDFReports.md

# PDF Reports Specification

## Objective

Generate professional, presentation-ready PDF reports for operational,
managerial and executive users.

## General Features

-   One-click generation
-   Company logo support
-   Hotel branding
-   Page numbering
-   Automatic date/time
-   Professional cover page
-   Table of contents
-   Charts embedded as vector graphics
-   Executive summary generated by AI
-   Export in A4 portrait and landscape

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Report Catalog

## 1. Executive Credit Report

Audience: - General Manager - Finance Director

Sections: - Executive Summary - Portfolio Overview - Key KPIs - DSO -
Recovery Rate - Cash Forecast - Top 10 Debtors - Critical Accounts -
Portfolio Risk - AI Recommendations - Action Plan

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Credit Manager Report

Includes: - Aging Balance - Collections - Credit Limits - Payment
Promises - Litigation - Risk Analysis - Customer Ranking - Trend
Analysis - Monthly Comparison

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Collection Report

Includes: - Calls - Emails - Promises - Payments - Success Rate -
Pending Actions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Cash Forecast Report

Periods: - 7 Days - 15 Days - 30 Days - 60 Days - 90 Days

Includes: - Expected Cash - Confidence Score - Forecast Variance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. DSO Report

Includes: - Current DSO - Monthly Trend - Yearly Trend - Benchmark - AI
Commentary

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Portfolio Risk Report

Includes: - Risk Matrix - High Risk Customers - Credit Exposure -
Litigation - Recommended Actions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Customer Report

Contains: - Customer Profile - Aging - Credit Limit - Payment History -
Reminder History - Litigation - AI Risk Summary

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Month-End Report

Includes: - Portfolio Evolution - KPI Evolution - Cash Position - AI
Executive Summary - Conclusions

------------------------------------------------------------------------
