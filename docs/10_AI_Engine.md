# AI Engine Specification

## Objective

Provide an intelligent assistant that continuously analyzes the hotel
receivables portfolio and recommends the best actions.

## AI Modules

### Portfolio Analysis

-   Overall portfolio health score
-   Portfolio summary in natural language
-   Daily executive briefing

### Customer Risk

Calculate: - Payment probability - Default probability - Risk score -
Recommended risk level

### Collection Assistant

Every day identify: - Customers to call - Customers to email - Customers
to block - Customers to unblock - Promises due today - New critical
accounts

### Cash Forecast

Forecast: - 7 days - 15 days - 30 days - 60 days - 90 days

Display: - Expected collections - Confidence score - Forecast variance

### DSO Prediction

Estimate: - End of month DSO - End of quarter DSO - End of year DSO

### Executive Summary

Generate automatically: - Portfolio status - Main risks - Positive
changes - Negative changes - Recommended actions

### Anomaly Detection

Detect: - Unusual balance increase - Duplicate customer - Missing
payment - Credit limit abuse - Abnormal payment behavior

### Smart Recommendations

Examples: - Increase credit limit - Reduce credit limit - Start
litigation - Escalate collection - Schedule meeting - Request guarantee

## AI Chat

Example prompts: - Which customers require immediate action? - Why did
DSO increase? - Forecast next month's collections. - Generate today's
Credit Manager report. - Explain portfolio risk.

## Learning

The engine stores: - User decisions - AI recommendations -
Recommendation acceptance - Forecast accuracy

Future versions may improve recommendations from historical behavior.

## AI Safety

AI never changes business data automatically. All recommendations
require user validation. Every accepted recommendation is logged in the
audit trail.
