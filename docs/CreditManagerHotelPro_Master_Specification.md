# Credit Manager Hotel Pro --- Master Specification

## Purpose

Create a premium Android application dedicated to Hotel Credit
Management.

## Includes

-   Excel aging balance import
-   Historical imports
-   Monthly/yearly comparisons
-   Executive dashboards
-   KPI engine (DSO, ADD, CEI, Recovery Rate, Cash Forecast,
    Exposure...)
-   Risk scoring
-   Credit block/unblock workflow
-   Collection management
-   Litigation
-   Payment promises
-   Alerts
-   AI recommendations
-   PDF/Excel/CSV exports
-   Multi-hotel and multi-company
-   Role-based dashboards

## Official Excel Model

Libellé \| 0-30 JRS \| 31-60 JRS \| 61-90 JRS \| 91-120 JRS \| AU DELÀ
120 JRS \| Total

glassmorphism, animated KPI cards, interactive charts, light/dark mode.

### Charts

Line, Area, Donut, Stacked Bar, Heat Map, Treemap, Waterfall, Gauge,

### Navigation

Dashboard \| Clients \| Analytics \| Reports \| More

## Architecture

Clean Architecture: presentation/ domain/ data/ core/

## Reports

Professional Credit Manager report with executive summary, KPI analysis,
trends, graphs, action plan, recommendations and exports (PDF, Excel,
CSV).

This document is the master specification. All code, UI, colors,
